Privacy Policy

App: Football Quiz: Guess the Player
Developer: Payasli Studio
Effective Date: July 1, 2025
Last Updated: July 1, 2025

Payasli Studio ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data is collected when you use the "Football Quiz: Guess the Player" mobile application ("the App") and how that data is used.

1. Data We Collect

The App itself does not collect or store any personally identifiable information on our servers. The following data is processed locally on your device:

  • Nickname you choose (stored locally, never sent to us)
  • Game progress, scores, and game history (stored locally)
  • Language and audio preferences (stored locally)

2. Third-Party Services

2.1 Google AdMob

The App displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect:

  • Advertising identifier (Android Advertising ID)
  • Approximate location (country-level, from IP address)
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • App usage data (ad views, clicks)

This data is used by Google to serve personalized ads and measure ad performance. Read Google's privacy policy here: https://policies.google.com/privacy

2.2 Wikipedia / Wikimedia Commons

Footballer photos are loaded on-demand from Wikimedia Commons under Creative Commons licenses. When your device requests a photo, Wikimedia may log your IP address temporarily. We do not control or store this data.

3. Permissions

The App requests only the INTERNET permission, which is required to:

  • Load footballer photos from Wikipedia
  • Display ads through AdMob

4. Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for all ages (PEGI 3 / Everyone). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us.

5. Data Retention

All local data (nickname, scores, preferences) remains on your device until you uninstall the App. Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data.

6. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data on our servers, there is no data for you to request, modify, or delete from us. For AdMob-related data, please refer to Google's privacy controls: https://myaccount.google.com/data-and-privacy

7. Disclaimer

This App is not officially affiliated with FIFA, UEFA, any football club, league, or player. All trademarks belong to their respective owners. Footballer names and biographical data are publicly available facts. Photos used under Creative Commons licenses are credited to original photographers.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be announced in the App's update notes.

9. Contact

For privacy questions, content removal requests, or any feedback:
Email: payaslistudio@gmail.com
